PRIVACY POLICY


By using Farm At Hand you agree to the following privacy policies outlined in this document.

Farm At Hand's Commitment to Online Privacy

Your right to privacy is important. At Farm At Hand we believe that personal information you choose to furnish online should only be used to help us provide you with the highest quality of products, services and support. We display this Privacy Statement to make you aware of the information practices for Farm At Hand websites, including what type of information is gathered and tracked, how the information is used, and with whom the information is shared. We may update our Privacy Statement from time to time to stay current with changes in our business and to better serve you. We encourage you to visit this page frequently to inform yourself of any changes.

Consent to Use and Transfer Personal Data

By submitting your personal data through a Farm At Hand web site you consent to Farm At Hand's collection, use, storage and transfer of your personal data to our company databases and other repositories, wherever located, for the purposes for which it is submitted and in accordance with the policies set forth in this Privacy Statement.

Types of Data Collected Online

There are areas of the Farm At Hand that may be accessed without logging in with a personal account. Only general, non-personally identifiable information that Farm At Hand logs from all site visits, such as Internet service provider, operating system/browser type, and pages accessed, may be collected in those instances. This means that a user's session may be tracked, but the user will not be individually identified, nor will that information ever be sold. That information will only be used to see the type of traffic our website is receiving and the improve our content and efficiency.

When registering for an account you may be asked for: name, email address, mailing address and/or phone number. This information will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other company for any reason whatsoever, without your consent.

Activity data is generated over the course of using the Farm At Hand service. This includes all the information that you enter into the app or website. This data is collected and stored and associated with your account only to provide you with the services of Farm At Hand. This data will nevery be sold, shared, exchanged, or transferred to a third party without your consent. If however you chose to share your account with other individuals we are not responsible for how data is shared by those individuals.

Use of Data

In the ordinary course of business Farm At Hand will not sell, trade or rent your individual identifying information to others adsent your consent to do so.

Farm At Hand may use the information you provide to notify you later about new developments with respect to the products and services you have purchased or to inform you about new products, special offers and services offered by us.

Personal Information is or may be used: (i) to provide and improve our Service, (ii) to administer your use of the Service, (iii) to better understand your needs and interests, (iv) to personalize and improve your experience, and (v) to provide or offer software updates and product announcements. If you no longer wish to receive communications from us, please contact us at info@farmathand.com to unsubscribe to such updates.

Analytics

Farm At Hand may also collect some information (ourselves or using third party services) using logging and cookies, such as IP address, which can sometimes be correlated with Personal Information. We use this information for the above purposes and to monitor and analyze use of the Service, for the Service's technical administration, to increase our Service's functionality and user-friendliness, and to verify users have the authorization needed for the Service to process their requests. As of the date this policy went into effect, we use Google Analytics. To learn more about the privacy policy of Google Analytics, click, and to learn how to opt out of that service click here.

Non-private and non-personal information

Farm At Hand may occasionally use non-identifiable non-personal information such a usage statistics, non-identifiable activity data with trusted third parties in an effort to better tailor our products, services, and advertisements to our users. Non-identifiable activity data may be analyzed for statistical information to better understand and improve our services.

Links

Please note that our websites contain links to other sites. When you access a third party's website, even one that may contain the Farm At Hand logo and content regarding Farm At Hand's products and services, please understand that the website is independent of Farm At Hand. We do not control the privacy policies of any third party sites. If you click-through a banner ad or link to a third party site and submit your information, you will be subject to the privacy policies of those third parties. We assume no responsibility or liability for any third party policies or the actions of third parties. A link to a third-party website is not an endorsement.

Security of your Personal Information

Farm At Hand secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Farm At Hand secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.
