About Us


2013 Young Promising Entrepreneur Award


2013 Most Promising Startup


2013 Best Ag App

Farm At Hand lets you know exactly what’s happening on your farm from anywhere at anytime. It gives you access to information and open lines of communication when you need it, allowing you to make the most informed decisions possible – saving you time and money. Farm At Hand is currently available for iOS, mobile web and web, coming soon to Android and Windows phone. Farm At Hand is taking farm management from the notebook to the cloud.

Farm At Hand is based in Saskatoon and Vancouver.



Kim Keller

kimkeller@farmathand.com


Kim Keller is a co-founder of Farm At Hand Inc., as well as a Saskatchewan producer. She brings first hand and practical knowledge of Ag to this project. Her involvement with her family’s farm gives her valuable insignt, which is applied to Farm At Hand. Her experience on the farm has informed the functionality, user interface and features. Kim is passionate about Ag and the future of the industry. It is important to her that Farm At Hand leads innovation in farm management positively affect change in the industry.


Himanshu Singh

himanshusingh@farmathand.com


Himanshu Singh is a co founder of Farm At Hand. His approach to development in both product and business is combining innovation with functionality. With Farm At Hand, his knowledge and experience in the tech field is used to bridge the gap between practical necessities of farm management with the mobile abilities of the tech industry. Being new to the Ag industry, Himanshu has been able to apply traditional business development strategies to this unique market, giving Farm At Hand a new perspective on Ag.
